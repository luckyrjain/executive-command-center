# Enterprise Compliance Contract

Each claimed control maps to owner, implementation, scope, test procedure, evidence, review cadence and exceptions. Marketing/compliance claims cannot exceed verified controls.

Audit events are append-only, time-synchronized, redacted and exportable with an integrity manifest. Retention and deletion follow published policy; active legal hold prevents deletion but not access control. Key rotation, backup restore, incident response, vulnerability management and access review produce dated evidence. Exceptions expire and require risk acceptance.
