# Phase 3 UX States

## Primary surfaces

Attention queue, waiting views, risk review queue, daily/weekly planner, plan conflict review and meeting preparation.

## Attention queue

Group by needs action, waiting on others, risks, upcoming meetings and safely deferred. Every item shows why, confidence, freshness and evidence. Pin, dismiss and defer are reversible. Scores are secondary to plain-language rationale.

## Planner

Provide timeline and accessible list views. Show capacity used, focus time, unscheduled work and conflicts. Drag-and-drop has keyboard equivalents. Proposed and accepted plans are unmistakable. Replanning presents a diff before acceptance.

## Meeting preparation

Separate facts, open questions and suggestions. Show citations inline and evidence gaps without alarming language. Stale packs remain readable with a refresh action and change summary.

## Required states

Loading, empty, no capacity, over capacity, conflict, stale sources, degraded/AI unavailable, offline, permission denied, recoverable error and version conflict. Deterministic functionality stays usable in degraded state.

## Accessibility and ethics

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Never use red/green alone, urgency animation, shame language or person leaderboards. Respect reduced motion and 200% zoom. All explanations and controls are keyboard and screen-reader accessible.
